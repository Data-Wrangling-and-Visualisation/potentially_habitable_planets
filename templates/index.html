Exoplanet Explorations

Exoplanets are planets that orbit stars outside our solar system. They come in various sizes, temperatures, and orbital characteristics. This visualization helps you explore their properties using interactive charts.

1. Planet Characteristics Comparison

The radar chart compares key characteristics of selected exoplanets, including mass, radius, flux, temperature, and distance from Earth. Use the dropdown menu to select planets for comparison.

2. Habitable Zone Orbit Visualization

The visualization shows the orbits of exoplanets around their host stars. The orbits are scaled based on the distance of the planet from Earth (Distance (ly)). Planets in the habitable zone are highlighted in green.

3. StarMap: Habitability Visualization

This interactive star map visualizes exoplanet systems with scientifically calculated habitability scores. Each star's color represents the average potential habitability of its planets, ranging from yellow to green.
